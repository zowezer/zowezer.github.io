## Design Context

### Users
People who seek natural, organic, chemical-free skincare products. Their main goal is to purchase authentic Aleppo soap and learn about its ancient history and purity.

### Brand Personality
Pure, Ancient, Natural, Olive Oil-based. 3 words: Authentic, Traditional, Purity.

### Aesthetic Direction
Earthy tones (olive green, sand, clay), organic textures, traditional yet refined e-commerce experience. Clean layout prioritizing the product's heritage and purity.

### Design Principles
1. **Purity First**: Use clean, minimalist layouts that reflect the chemical-free nature of the product.
2. **Heritage Driven**: Incorporate visual elements that evoke the ancient soap-making tradition of Aleppo.
3. **Organic Palette**: Stick to a natural, earthy color scheme derived from olive oil and laurel.
4. **Tactile Typography**: Use a distinctive serif for headings to feel established and "ancient", paired with a legible sans-serif for body text.
